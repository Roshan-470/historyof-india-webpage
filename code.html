19th Century

🌍 19th Century Major Historical Events (1801–1900)

  


⚔ Wars & Revolutions


    
  
  • Napoleonic Wars (1803–1815): Europe was dominated by Napoleon’s campaigns, ending with the Battle of Waterloo.
    • 
  
  • Latin American Wars of Independence (1808–1833): Mexico, Argentina, Colombia gained independence from Spain/Portugal.
    • 
  
  • Greek War of Independence (1821–1829): Greece gained freedom from the Ottoman Empire.
    • 
  
  • Revolutions of 1848: A wave of liberal uprisings across Europe.
    • 
  
  • American Civil War (1861–1865): Union vs Confederacy — slavery & states' rights.
    • 
  
  • Franco-Prussian War (1870–1871): Unification of Germany.
    • 




🏛 Political and Social Reforms


    
  
  • Abolition of Slavery: Britain (1833), USA (1865), Brazil (1888).
    • 
  
  • Unification: Germany (1871), Italy (1861).
    • 
  
  • Nationalism: Rising national identity.
    • 
  
  • Women's Rights Movement: Early campaigns begin.
    • 




⚙ Industrial Revolution & Inventions


    
  
  • Second Industrial Revolution: Steel, electricity, mass production.
    • 
  
  • Inventions: Telephone, Light bulb, Typewriter, Engine, Railways.
    • 
  
  • Urbanization: Massive shift to cities.
    • 




🧪 Scientific Discoveries


    
  
  • Charles Darwin: Theory of Evolution.
    • 
  
  • Louis Pasteur: Germ theory, vaccines.
    • 
  
  • Michael Faraday: Electromagnetism.
    • 




🌐 Imperialism & Global Expansion


    
  
  • British Empire: Largest in history.
    • 
  
  • Scramble for Africa: European colonization.
    • 
  
  • Opium Wars: British dominance in China.
    • 
  
  • Meiji Restoration: Japan's modernization.
    • 




📚 Cultural Movements


    
  
  • Romanticism: Poets like Byron, Shelley, Wordsworth.
    • 
  
  • Realism: Realistic art/literature.
